Her name is Maria Jose Cristerna Mendez. She is well known as "La Mujer Vampiro," The Vampire Woman, though she prefers to be called The Jaguar Woman. She was listed in Guinness World Records book in 2012 as "the most modified female" in the world, with 96% of her skin is tattooed and 49 body implants, including a range of transdermal implants on her forehead, chest, arms, as well as multiple piercings in many parts of her body.

Maria Jose Cristerna Mendez, "The Vampire Woman."

She went to the Catholic University of Mexico and graduated with a major in criminal law. She became a victim of domestic violence in her marriage which led her to decide to modify her body parts as a sign of strength, courage, and liberation. She is also a businesswoman who owns a tattoo studio and a boutique where she sells her clothing line called "Mujeres Vampiro", meaning Vampire Women.
