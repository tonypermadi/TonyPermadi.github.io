Top Social Media

This is an article about top rated social media apps on Tony.Permadi.id website. It contains the top ten list of Android social media applications ranked by Statista.com and also rated by millions of people who downloaded them from Google Play Store.

Since 2008 (14 years ago), Google Play has served as the official app store for certified Android devices. It currently has 3.48 million Android applications ready to be downloaded by anyone, except the Chinese. Yes, many western apps are banned in China and the Chinese made their own app versions.

By looking at the following Statista.com's list of the top 10 social media used by the people of this planet, we can see that five of them are made in China specially made for the Chinese.

1. Facebook.

Monthly Active Users: 2.910 billion people.

Google Play Store Rating: 3.1 ★
Reviewed by 129 million people.

Categorized as: Social App.     Rated: 13+.

Developed by: Meta Platforms, Inc. (USA).

First Launched: Feb 4, 2004 (18 years ago).

Latest App Release: July 9, 2022.

Latest App Version: 374.0.0.20.109.

App Description:
Facebook app helps you stay connected with friends and family. The app can be used as an organizer for storing, saving, and also sharing photos straight from the Android camera. You have total control over all your photos, privacy settings, and setting up a secret photo album.

Facebook also helps you keep up with updated news and events around the world. Subscribe to favorite artists, celebrities, news sources, or sports teams to follow their newsfeeds or live streaming videos and be caught up on the latest happenings no matter where you are.

The most important desktop features of Facebook are also available on the app, such as writing on timelines, liking photos, browsing for people, and editing your profile and groups.

8. Douyin.

Monthly Active Users: 600 million people.

Google Play Store Rating: 4.4 ★
Reviewed by 15 million people.

Categorized as: Social.     Rated: 12+.

Developed by: ByteDance Ltd. (China).

First Launched: Sep 2016 (6 years ago).

Latest App Release: July 12, 2022.

Latest App Version: 21.5.0.

App Description:
Douyin app is the TikTok app for the Chinese, especially for the people living in China. In the Asian countries many social networks are limited, and the case of TikTok is no different. In this way, they prevent content from the rest of the world from being accessible in China.

The working of Douyin app is very similar to its international version of TikTok. Users will find millions of new short videos everyday and they can record, edit, and share all kinds of short videos. Users can also use filters, hundreds of stickers, and many editing options in the app.

In Douyin, the short videos its users watch are adapted to their habits and tastes. They can also discover new content and creators, and the possibility to send messages to other users just like any messaging applications.

App Description:
Weibo is a Chinese microblogging app just like Twitter. Launched for the first time on August 14, 2009 by Sina Corporation, it is one of the biggest social media platforms in China, with over 573 million monthly active users and 252 million daily active users as of quarter 1 2022.

With Weibo, the Chinese can discover hot new stuff at their fingertips, catch the trendy topics about movies, music, sports, traveling, fashion, get closer to their favorite idols, find the story behind the scenes, explore their interests and share anything they want at anytime anywhere.

The content of Weibo which support all kinds of media such as photos, GIFs, text, video clips and emoji can also be easily shared to Twitter, Facebook, and other social media platforms.
