Robyn Rihanna Fenty

Today, February 20, is the birthday of Robyn Rihanna Fenty who was born in year of 1988. Famous as RIHANNA, this Barbadian musician moved to the USA in 2005 from her hometown of Bridgetown in Barbados. In 2007, her "Umbrella" song earned her a Grammy. Know more about Rihanna!

Grammy Award

Grammy Award is a well-known international award to acknowledge achievements of music industry people. The award was originally called Gramophone Award and was held for the first time in 1959. Grammy Award trophy is a gilded gramophone. More about the Grammy Award.
