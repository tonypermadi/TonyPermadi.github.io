According to Statista, there are more than 2.87 million Android applications available in Google Play Store as of March 2020. With 81.87 million mobile users of the 267-million population of Indonesia, one out of three has access to smartphones. But which Android apps are useful for them? Here is the list of top 10 Android applications we think Indonesians must install in their devices.

The following Android applications are sorted according to the order of A to Z instead of the most used applications by Indonesian people.
